About

Hi—I'm Anna Lisi, an editorial & graphic designer and managing editor based in Washington, DC. I bring 20+ years of experience leading communications and publication design for global development, health, and industry organizations. I partner with businesses, publishers, nonprofits, and startups to produce magazines, annual reports, white papers, and brand systems that read clearly and look sharp.

Experience highlights:

  • Palladium: Make It Possible (Nov 2016–Mar 2025): Senior Communications Manager & Managing Editor. Led multi‑year publications, campaigns, and brand systems across global health and development programs.
  • FHI 360 — FANTA (Oct 2012–Oct 2016): Communications Manager & Managing Editor for technical nutrition and health publications, research briefs, and toolkits.
  • JSI — AIDSTAR‑One (Jun 2010–Oct 2012): Communications Manager for USAID’s global HIV/AIDS technical publications and knowledge products.
  • American Chemistry Council (2003–May 2010): Communications Manager; Managing Editor of American Chemistry magazine and Assistant Editor for Chemistry Business.

I combine editorial judgment (clear narratives, rigorous editing) with design systems (grids, typography, data visualization) to deliver content that resonates with decision‑makers, funders, and the public.

Tools: Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, and Figma. Turnkey production with printers and digital vendors on request.
